About ZipOps

ZipOps aims to be the reference of deployment platform, production ready application and open source tools.
Thanks to our experience with the diverse existent product like AWS, Google Cloud or Heroku we saw that they had a complicated interface, proprietary tools, data host in another country than France and expensive price.

ZipOps want to propose a platform with an easy interface to deploy your application and a suite of open source tools like Teraform, Gitlab, and more.

The project is developed by a EPITECH student group for their Epitech Inovating Project
